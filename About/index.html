Hardware Design Engineer, Firmware and Software Enthusiast

I have two years of experience in hardware electronics design and software engineering. I enjoy discovering, learning, and understanding new technologies to find interesting ways of using them.

I usually find areas of technology that I have not been exposed to and try to understand their fundamentals through practice, or creating a project to complete. A good showcase of what I am currently thinking about would be on the Projects tab.

I also enjoy puzzles and games as a way of challenging my creativity and lateral thinking. A recent favourite is variant sudoku (killer, thermo, and GAS - daily random approachable puzzles.) I’d also try a cryptic crossword once in a while if there’s a newspaper around. As for games, recent interesting ones were Tunic, Chants of Sennaar, and Outer Wilds.
